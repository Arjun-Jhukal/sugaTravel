Overview

Siklis is one of the largest villages in the Gurung group. And it still reflects the people’s traditional way of life. This lovely town sits above the Mardi Khola at an elevation of 1981 meters. With views of Annapurna IV to the north and Annapurna II and Lamjung Himal to the east. Siklis was known for its pristine forested surroundings. Making it an ideal location for bird watchers, as Siklis Trek is home to a variety of bird species.

Itinerary

01 Day

Drive from Kathmandu to Pokhara, overnight at lodging.

After breakfast, at a young hour early in the day you will touch base at Pokhara after a drive for about 6 hours. You will have the capacity to have a stunning display of stream and view completely through your drive. You will be taken to your lodging and with an unwinding evening. You can even visit around lakeside area or you can go for sculling at well known Fewa Lake. We will remain overnight in the lodging.
